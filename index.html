Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer at BrightPath Accounting?

At BrightPath Accounting, we specialize in tax return preparation and comprehensive bookkeeping services tailored for both small businesses and individual clients. Our experienced team ensures you receive personalized attention and expert financial guidance to meet your unique needs.

How do I schedule an appointment for tax preparation?

You can easily schedule an appointment by visiting our contact page or calling us directly at our office. We operate 100% online offering flexible scheduling options to accommodate your busy lifestyle through virtual consultations.

What documents do I need to bring for my tax preparation?

To ensure a smooth and efficient tax preparation process, please provide relevant documents such as W-2s, 1099s, other income records, previous tax returns, and any receipts for deductions you may want to claim. Our team will guide you on any additional documents needed during the preparation.

How do your bookkeeping services work?

Our bookkeeping services are designed to simplify your financial management. We offer monthly and quarterly bookkeeping packages, and provide detailed financial statements. We use cloud-based software, through QuickBooks Online, allowing you to access your financial information whenever you need it.
